About Us

CompuTech Nexus Academy is a premier computer training center established in 2024 by a committee of three individuals: Engr. Shamsu Sabo, Engr. Kamal Auwal Ibrahim, and Jazuli Adam. The academy is dedicated to equipping individuals with the skills needed to excel in today's digital landscape. Our team of experienced directors and instructors is committed to providing high-quality education and fostering technological innovation.

Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals with practical skills and knowledge in the field of computer technology, enabling them to succeed in their careers and contribute positively to society.

Vision

Our vision is to become a leading institution in computer education, recognized for excellence in teaching, research, and technological innovation, both nationally and internationally.
